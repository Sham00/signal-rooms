What to Watch
NVDA next earnings date & guidance tone — management's data center revenue outlook and H200/B300 architecture ramp commentary are the primary sector catalyst each quarter.
H100 / B200 GPU lead times — hyperscaler delivery windows act as a real-time demand proxy; shortening lead times signal softening appetite before earnings confirm it.
Hyperscaler capex commentary — META, MSFT, AMZN, and GOOGL earnings calls; any capex guidance revision moves the entire AI infrastructure trade. Watch for "digestion" language.
TSMC monthly sales report — chips-sold data provides a leading demand signal one quarter ahead of semiconductor company earnings; sustained growth confirms AI build-out thesis.
US-China export controls — any new restrictions on advanced chips (A100/H100/B200 equivalents) directly impact NVDA, AMAT, and ASML addressable market sizing and guidance.
SOX index direction vs. broader market (beta) — beta expansion signals risk-on rotation into semis; compression indicates sector-specific headwinds decoupling from broader S&P 500 moves.
Memory pricing — HBM3e scarcity — High-Bandwidth Memory for AI training is supply-constrained; HBM price prints affect NVDA gross margins and SK Hynix / Micron relative leverage.
SMCI auditor risk — watch for restatement announcements or SEC correspondence; a fresh audit failure could reprice the entire AI server infrastructure basket.
